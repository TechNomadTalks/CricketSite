About Action Soccer and Cricket Arena

Port Shepstone's premier sports facility

Our Story

Building Sports Excellence

Action Soccer and Cricket Arena was founded with a vision to provide world-class sports facilities for soccer and cricket enthusiasts in Port Shepstone and surrounding areas.

With state-of-the-art pitches, professional maintenance, and a passion for both soccer and cricket, we've become the go-to destination for sports enthusiasts in Port Shepstone. Our facilities meet international standards and have hosted numerous memorable matches and training sessions for both sports.

We believe in making sports accessible to everyone, which is why we've developed an easy online booking system with transparent pricing at R500/hour and instant confirmation. The arena can accommodate up to 16 people (8 per side for cricket) in a professional environment.
